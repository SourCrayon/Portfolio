About me

Management skills

Through experience and workshops, I have developed a great sense for leadership and management. I can guide a team through the planning, execution and conclusion of our project, overcoming the challenges and difficulties that we meet along the way.

Adaptability

Changes are unavoidable and things don’t always go according to plan. I can adapt and change according to the needs of the project so we can find the most efficient way to keep progressing. Change is difficult but it can be very rewarding too!

Arts & Crafts

I am a digital artist, but I have also a deep rooted attachment to the traditional crafts as well. I am a self taught knitter, on my way to learn crochet and macrame as well. I understand the need for a focus on learning the techniques first and then adapting them to a bigger project that requires mixing a few skills together.

Team Player

As a team leader, I grew to understand how to make good use of everybody’s strengths while keeping them interested and motivated. I am of the opinion that our differences make us stronger and that variety can help us grow on an individual basis and as a whole.
